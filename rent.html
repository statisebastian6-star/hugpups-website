Rent a Therapy Dog

Bring comfort and companionship into your home for a week

How It Works

Our Rent a Dog service lets you welcome one of our trained therapy dogs into your home for a full week. It's a wonderful way to experience the comfort of a dog companion, especially if you're going through a hard time, supporting your mental health, or thinking about future pet ownership.

Each rental includes everything you need: food, supplies, a quick orientation, and 24/7 support from our team. Once your application is reviewed and approved, we'll match you with a dog whose temperament fits your needs.
