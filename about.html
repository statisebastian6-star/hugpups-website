About HugPups

Dedicated to improving lives through the power of therapy dogs

Our Mission

HugPups is a start-up therapy dog service. Our goal is to help people who are struggling with their mental health by connecting them with trained companion dogs. We believe everyone deserves the comfort that animals bring - even if they don't have the resources to own one themselves.

Our service isn't a substitute for therapy - it's an addition to it. We help people living with anxiety and depression to ease their symptoms, and give people experience with animals so they can hopefully own a pet of their own one day.

Our Values

Compassion

We approach every interaction with empathy and care, both for our clients and our dogs.

Excellence

Our therapy dogs meet the highest standards of training, certification and animal welfare.

Accessibility

We strive to make therapy dog companionship available to anyone who could benefit from it.
