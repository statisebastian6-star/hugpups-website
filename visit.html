About Our Office

Our office is a peaceful, accessible space designed with comfort in mind. Visit us to meet our therapy dogs and the team who looks after them. It's the perfect first step before applying to rent a dog, or just a relaxing visit on its own.

Sessions usually last 30-60 minutes. Whether you're coming alone or with friends or family, our handlers will guide your visit and make sure everyone feels welcome.
