Welcome to HugPups

At HugPups we believe in the healing power of the human-animal bond. Our certified therapy dogs and professional handlers provide emotional support, reduce stress, and bring joy to people who need it most.

Our service is not a substitute for therapy, but a meaningful addition to it. We help people dealing with anxiety and depression by providing companionship, while also giving them experience with animals so they can one day own a pet themselves.

Benefits of Therapy for Mental Health

Reduces Stress & Anxiety

Therapy dogs lower cortisol levels and help promote a sense of calm and relaxation.

Boosts Mood & Happiness

Spending time with a friendly dog raises serotonin and brings lasting joy.

Provides Emotional Support

Therapy dogs offer unconditional companionship and improve emotional resilience.

Pet Ownership Advice

Is a Therapy Dog Right for You?

Before renting a therapy dog, think about your lifestyle, schedule and ability to care for an animal. Renting is a great way to get experience with dogs before committing to permanent ownership.

Preparing for a Pet
