1. Introduction

Welcome to HugPups. These Terms and Conditions govern your use of our therapy dog services, including our Rent a Dog and Visit the Team services. By using any of our services, you agree to be bound by these terms.

2. Service Description

HugPups provides certified therapy dog services, including:

  • One week therapy dog rentals (Rent a Dog)
  • On-site therapy dog visits at our office (Visit the Team)
  • Information and advice on pet ownership and mental health

Our services are designed to support mental health and wellbeing. They are not a substitute for professional therapy or medical care.

3. User Responsibilities

When working with a therapy dog, you agree to:

  • Treat the therapy dog with kindness and respect at all times
  • Follow all instructions provided by HugPups staff
  • Maintain a safe environment for the dog
  • Respect the dog's wellbeing and not overstimulate them
  • Report any concerns immediately to our team

4. Rent a Dog Service

  • A completed application and approval are required
  • Rentals are for one week (7 days) and for individual use only
  • All food, supplies and care instructions are provided
  • A refundable deposit is required for the rental period
  • Sub-letting or transferring the rental is not permitted

5. Visit the Team Service

  • Visits must be booked in advance via the website
  • Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before the visit
  • Late arrivals may result in a shortened session
  • A handler will be present for the whole visit

6. Health and Safety

  • All our dogs are certified by recognised therapy dog organisations
  • Dogs receive regular veterinary care and health checks
  • Clients must disclose any allergies or relevant health conditions
  • HugPups may cancel sessions for safety reasons at any time

7. Liability and Insurance

8. Privacy and Confidentiality

We maintain strict confidentiality of all personal information. Client details are never shared without explicit consent, except where required by law. Your data is handled in line with UK GDPR.

9. Mental Health Disclaimer

Our services are designed as a supplement to, not a replacement for, professional mental health care. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a qualified medical professional or call 999 in an emergency.

10. Termination of Service

HugPups reserves the right to refuse or end a service at any time if the welfare of the therapy dog is at risk or if any client behaviour is deemed inappropriate.
